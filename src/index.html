Film-It

Full stack project. A website targeting media production students, to aid in helping them recruit people to their projects, by allowing them sign up for account, post ad for their projects, a feed for people to look at projects posted, simple messaging integrated into website, dashboard to track post and applications. Some notable features: User accounts (auth0), messaging (socket.io), CRUD(Mongodb). Stack: Mongodb, nodejs, react, socket.io
